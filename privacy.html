Privacy Policy

My Match Mentors

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

My Match Mentors collects and processes the following information:

  • Photos: When you use our facial compatibility analysis feature, you may upload photos of yourself and potential matches. These photos are processed temporarily for analysis and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for technical support and app optimization.
  • Usage Analytics: We collect anonymous usage data to improve our app functionality and user experience.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide facial compatibility analysis services
  • To improve our app functionality and user experience
  • To provide technical support and customer service
  • To ensure app security and prevent fraud

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities:

  • Photos are processed securely and are not stored permanently
  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties

4. Camera and Photo Library Access

My Match Mentors requires access to your device's camera and photo library to:

  • Take photos for facial analysis
  • Select existing photos from your library
  • Process images for compatibility analysis

You can control these permissions through your device settings at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party services for:

  • Facial recognition and analysis (Google ML Kit)
  • App analytics and crash reporting
  • Cloud storage and processing

These services are bound by their own privacy policies and data protection standards.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt out of analytics collection
  • Control app permissions through device settings

7. Children's Privacy

My Match Mentors is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@mymatchmentors.com

Website: mymatchmentors.com

This privacy policy is effective as of January 2025 and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future.
