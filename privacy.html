Privacy Policy

My Match Mentors

Last Updated: August 2025

1. Information We Collect

My Match Mentors collects and processes the following information:

  • Photos: When you use our facial compatibility analysis feature, you may upload photos of yourself and potential matches. These photos are processed temporarily for analysis and are not stored permanently on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide facial compatibility analysis services

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities:

  • Photos are processed securely and are not stored permanently
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties
  • All processing occurs locally on your device

4. Photo Library Access

My Match Mentors requires access to your device's photo library to:

  • Select existing photos from your library
  • Process images for facial compatibility analysis

You can control these permissions through your device settings at any time.

5. Third-Party Services

Our app uses the following third-party service:

  • ML Kit: We use Google's ML Kit for facial detection and analysis. ML Kit processes data locally on your device. Please review Google's privacy policy for ML Kit.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Control app permissions through device settings
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the app.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@mymatchmentors.com

Website: mymatchmentors.com

This privacy policy is effective as of August 2025 and will remain in effect except with respect to any changes in its provisions in the future.
